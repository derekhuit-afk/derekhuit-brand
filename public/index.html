Background

The Story

Born in Grants Pass, Oregon. Based in Anchorage, Alaska. I've spent nearly two decades in mortgage lending — originating over $1 billion in loans, managing branches, recruiting loan officers, and building teams from Alaska to Florida.


In 2023, I started building what became Huit.AI — a full ecosystem of AI-powered tools designed to solve the problems I've lived with for 18 years. No one was building the technology mortgage professionals actually needed, so I built it myself. From a single HMDA data pipeline to 50 deployed products, every tool in the Huit.AI ecosystem comes from real-world lending experience, not a Silicon Valley pitch deck.


I hold an active NMLS mortgage license (#203980), a Business Administration degree from Northwest Nazarene University, and I operate an Airbnb property in Anchorage where I hold Superhost status. Beyond mortgage and tech, I'm researching and writing a nonfiction book about the Mona Lisa's chain of custody.
